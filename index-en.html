Who I Am

I am Cahyo Faizz Khafidzi, a proactive Digital Business undergraduate student at Universitas Negeri Surabaya (class of 2023) with a strong passion for the creative industry and digital marketing. My experience as the Chief Executive for a Business Model Canvas (BMC) competition and an entrepreneurship webinar has honed my skills in leading teams, end-to-end project management, and ensuring successful event execution.

I possess a solid technical skill set in content production, including graphic design, video and photo editing, photography, and the fundamentals of web and mobile app development.
